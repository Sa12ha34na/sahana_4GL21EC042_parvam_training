History of kodagu

kodagu is known for its "brave warriors". kodagu has a rich history, which can be experienced in Madikere. kodagu is also known for its waterfalls,forets,plantation and orange groves. kodagu is the birth place of kaveri River. kodagu is home to the native speakers of kodava languge.
